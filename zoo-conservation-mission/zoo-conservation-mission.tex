\begin{abstract}
\noindent The October 31, 2021 Zoo whitepaper established the Zoo
Foundation as the conservation $\times$ education arm of the Zoo
ecosystem. Four years later, this paper documents the on-chain
realization: the protocol-level sustainability tax, treasury custody
under M-Chain MPC, conservation grant cycles via the \DAO{}, the V1
species catalogue with their named non-profit partners, and the
post-quantum-secure settlement layer that anchors every grant
disbursement. We avoid restating the vision; instead, we list what
shipped, when, and against which 2021 commitment. This paper is the
canonical conservation-pillar artifact for 2025-12-15.
\end{abstract}
