\subsection{Hybrid AMM + CLOB}

Zoo DEX operates as a hybrid exchange with two execution venues:

\begin{figure}[t]
\centering
\begin{tikzpicture}[
    node distance=1.5cm,
    box/.style={rectangle, draw, minimum width=2.5cm, minimum height=0.9cm, align=center, fill=blue!10, font=\small},
    arrow/.style={->, thick}
]
    \node[box] (order) {User Order};
    \node[box, below left=1.5cm and 0.5cm of order] (amm) {AMM Pools\\(LP-9010)};
    \node[box, below right=1.5cm and 0.5cm of order] (clob) {CLOB\\(LP-9020)};
    \node[box, below=3.5cm of order] (router) {Smart Router\\(LP-9012)};
    \node[box, below=of router] (settle) {Settlement\\(LP-9030 Vault)};

    \draw[arrow] (order) -- (amm) node[midway, left, font=\tiny] {Liquidity};
    \draw[arrow] (order) -- (clob) node[midway, right, font=\tiny] {Price discovery};
    \draw[arrow] (amm) -- (router);
    \draw[arrow] (clob) -- (router);
    \draw[arrow] (router) -- (settle);
\end{tikzpicture}
\caption{Zoo DEX hybrid architecture. The smart router splits orders across AMM pools and the CLOB for optimal execution.}
\label{fig:architecture}
\end{figure}

\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{AMM Pools} (LP-9010): Concentrated liquidity pools (Uniswap V3-style) for deep, passive liquidity. Liquidity providers deposit token pairs and earn fees.

    \item \textbf{CLOB} (LP-9020): GPU-accelerated order book for active market makers. Supports limit orders, market orders, and stop-loss orders.

    \item \textbf{Smart Router} (LP-9012): Splits orders across both venues to minimize slippage. The router evaluates:
    \begin{equation}
    \text{split}^* = \arg\min_{x \in [0, 1]} \text{Slippage}(x \cdot \text{order}, \text{AMM}) + \text{Slippage}((1-x) \cdot \text{order}, \text{CLOB})
    \end{equation}
\end{itemize}
