\section{Native Token}

\subsection{Gold and Silver Backed Token}

The Zoo Token (\$ZOO) forms the core of the Zoo ecosystem, distinctively characterized by its unique backing of gold and silver. It is not merely a digital asset but a veritable gateway to the physical commodities realm, creating an innovative fusion of traditional and crypto assets.

The Zoo Token's distinctiveness starts with its multiplicity of liquidity pools. Unlike traditional tokens that are often confined to specific pools, Zoo Token's liquidity transcends typical boundaries, promoting accessibility and versatility for token holders. The founding members of Zoo Game will launch the largest liquidity pools, which will contain Lux Tokens exclusively. These Lux Tokens, acting as a representation of real-world commodities, will be backed by silver and gold, infusing tangible value and stability into the digital Zoo ecosystem.

Moreover, the flexibility of transactions with these Lux Tokens is key to understanding their value. Token holders have the liberty to transfer, sell, or even redeem these tokens to acquire their physical counterparts. In essence, the Zoo Token unlocks a pathway to an assortment of natural assets and commodities.

While the initial phase will focus on precious metals, there is immense potential to broaden this scope, encompassing a wider array of valuable resources. This expansion will further diversify and enrich the commodities within the ecosystem, amplifying the overall value of the Zoo ecosystem.

The Zoo Token embodies a bridging function between virtual and real-world assets. This pioneering step blurs the lines between the digital and physical worlds, bringing a unique dimension of trading precious metals within the virtual Zoo ecosystem. Furthermore, it upholds a critical role within the game, driving the gameplay and heightening the utility of the platform.

\subsection{Acquisition Process}

Through an inventive bridge feature, the Zoo Token can be purchased with almost any asset, facilitating users to effortlessly transform their existing holdings into Zoo Tokens. This level of convenience encourages a wider demographic of participants to engage within the Zoo ecosystem, thereby fostering inclusivity.

Zoo Labs capitalizes on gold-backed token expertise to design a solid framework for the implementation of commodity-backed tokens. The incorporation of gold and silver backing significantly bolsters the utility and stability of the Zoo Token. This unique feature caters to a spectrum of participants, including crypto enthusiasts seeking innovation and traditional investors seeking tangible assets with a secure store-of-value function.

The unique characteristics of the Zoo Token, primarily its gold and silver backing, combined with its role as the Zoo Game's native token, make it an exceptional digital asset in the field. It provides a platform for users to immerse themselves within the Zoo ecosystem, convert assets seamlessly via the bridge, and interact with a diverse array of commodities. It exemplifies how the digital world can integrate with the physical, underpinning the next level of innovation in the intersection of gaming and finance.
