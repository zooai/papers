\section{Feeding, Growing, and Breeding}

In the realm of Zoo Labs, the journey of NFT animals extends far beyond their acquisition. They require care, growth, and reproduction to thrive in their digital habitat.

\subsection{Feeding}

Feeding assumes paramount importance in raising NFT animals within Zoo Labs. By providing them with sustenance, owners facilitate their growth and enhance their value. Feeding involves allocating collateral, symbolizing the owner's dedication to the animal's development.

When a baby animal is nourished, it undergoes a transformation into a teenage specimen, increasing its value in the process. Similarly, nurturing a teenager to adulthood elevates its desirability within the NFT marketplace. The collateral invested during feeding strengthens the robustness of the Zoo Labs ecosystem.

\subsection{Cultivating}

As NFT animals progress through various growth stages, their appearance and attributes undergo a remarkable metamorphosis. From adorable infants to magnificent adults, each phase grants unique characteristics and heightens their value. Through nourishment and cultivation, owners wield the power to shape the trajectory of their animals' growth. The collateral allocated during this process unlocks the animals' full potential.

\subsection{Breeding}

Breeding is a pivotal feature of Zoo Labs, empowering collectors to create new and extraordinary NFT animals. By pairing two adult animals of the same species, users can generate an egg that holds the promise of a rare and exceptional creature. Each generation possesses distinct breeding privileges. Initial generation animals enjoy the most significant breeding allowances, enabling them to produce offspring up to seven times. However, with each subsequent generation, the breeding privileges decrease by one. This limitation ensures a balanced and sustainable breeding ecosystem within Zoo Labs.
