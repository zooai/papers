\section{The Lux-Family Triumvirate on \Zoo{}}
\label{sec:triumvirate}

A \Lux{}-family L1 is \emph{triumvirate-complete} when it carries
three first-class subsystems: a native decentralised exchange
(\DEX{}), a Cancun-equivalent Ethereum Virtual Machine (\textsc{evm}),
and a fully-homomorphic encryption substrate (\FHE{}). Networks lacking
any of the three are toy. \Zoo{} 4.0 is the third network in the
family to ship the triumvirate, after \Lux{} 4.0 and \Hanzo{} 4.0
(all on 2026-02-14), and the first purpose-built around conservation,
wildlife \NFT{}s, and \DAO{}-governed funding.

\subsection{\DEX{} on \Zoo{}}

The \Zoo{} \DEX{} (\S\ref{sec:zoo-dex}) is a native \AMM{} pair in V2
(constant product) and V3 (concentrated liquidity), with V4 as a
precompile interface to the \Lux{} \DEX{}. Liquidity Protocol
integration follows in the April 20, 2026 launch wave, exposing
\Zoo{} pools to the cross-chain settlement router shared with \Lux{}
D-Chain, \Hanzo{} HMM, and Pars PARSWAP.

\subsection{\textsc{evm} on \Zoo{}}

\Zoo{} 4.0 inherits the \Lux{}-family \GPU{}-native \textsc{evm}
(\textsc{lp-009}). \textsc{evm} bytecode evaluation runs as
deterministic \GPU{} kernels through the \QuasarGPU{} adapter
(\textsc{lp-132}) under the \GPU{}-Residency Invariant
(\textsc{lp-137}). Per-block parallelism is materially higher than a
CPU \textsc{evm} of the same gas configuration; the public benchmark
of $360{,}000$ TPS sustained on \Lux{} C-Chain at $h_0$ applies
identically to \Zoo{} L1 since the kernels are shared.

The \Zoo{} \textsc{evm} adds three precompiles specific to
conservation/securities use cases: confidential ERC-20 (via
\textsc{lp-067}), \NFT{}-fractional vault aggregator, and \DAO{}
holographic-consensus weight evaluation. All three are \GPU{}-resident.

\subsection{\FHE{} on \Zoo{}}

\Zoo{} 4.0 reaches the \FHE{} substrate through F-Chain
(\textsc{lp-013}). Three application patterns activate at $h_0$:

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1.2em]
  \item Confidential \NFT{} bids (price hidden, ownership public).
  \item Private \DAO{} voting on conservation grants
        (\S\ref{sec:privacy}).
  \item Confidential ERC-20 transfers for high-value collections
        (\textsc{lp-067}).
\end{itemize}

The triumvirate is therefore complete: \DEX{} provides the venue,
\textsc{evm} provides the logic, \FHE{} provides the confidentiality.
The remainder of this paper is structured around how each piece is
realised on \Zoo{} L1 and how the four-network family co-activates on
2026-02-14.
