\section{Overview}


This document is the authoritative checklist for \Zoo{} Network mainnet launch.
It covers pre-launch validation, rehearsal on staging, launch-day sequence,
post-launch operations, and rollback procedures.

\Zoo{} Network is a Layer~2 on \Lux{} mainnet comprising 14 virtual machines
(chains) and one Layer~3 subnet (Beluga). All chains share \Lux{} consensus
and settle to \Lux{} P-Chain.

\medskip
\noindent\textbf{Clusters:}
\begin{itemize}[nosep]
  \item \texttt{lux-k8s} --- \Lux{} mainnet validators (DOKS, SFO3)
  \item \texttt{lux-test-k8s} --- Testnet / staging (DOKS, SFO3)
  \item \texttt{hanzo-k8s} --- Shared platform services (IAM, KMS, gateway)
\end{itemize}

\noindent\textbf{Status legend:}
\pending\ = pending,
\done\ = complete,
\blocked\ = blocked.
