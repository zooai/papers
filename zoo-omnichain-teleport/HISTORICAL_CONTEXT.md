# Canonical Zoo Historical Context for Bridge / Token / Omnichain Papers

## Source: Original Oct 2021 + Jan 2022 $ZOO Deck PDFs

### Zoo Bridge Origins (Oct 2021 Q4 Roadmap)
- Zoo Bridge was in the **original Oct 2021 Q4 roadmap**: "Bridge between ETH and Binance"
- BSC bridge was the **first one planned**

### Zoo Bridge Launch (Jan 2022 Roadmap)
- January 2022 roadmap confirmed: **February 2022 = "ZOO Bridge" launch**
- Original Jan 2022 deck description: "Our bridge allows each users to play ZOO on any chain. Creates a secure platform to exchange $ZOO for other assets"
- Listed asset icons: NEM, NEO, Dash, Litecoin, BitcoinCash, Zcash, Steem, Decred, Ethereum, XRP, EOS, Monero, Bitcoin, TRON, Dogecoin, Tether

### Zoo NFT Origins
- Zoo NFTs started as **ERC-721 on BSC** (PancakeSwap integration for $ZOO and egg purchases)
- "Contains 1 of 16 Generation one Base Animals" — original collection was **16 generation-one base animals**, expanded to 20 in later materials

### Yield-Transferability via NFT Ownership (Original Innovation)
- Yield transferable via NFT ownership — **token-bound-account predecessor pattern**
- "NFT yield and collateral is transferable to new owners until the Animal is freed from ZOO"
- Freeing = burn = realize
- ZRC-721 spec should preserve this **yield-transferability-until-burn** pattern from the 2022 deck

### Historical Significance
- The Zoo Bridge (2022) **predates most cross-chain bridges** and was one of the earliest "play on any chain" NFT bridges
- The Zoo Jan 2022 "ZOO Bridge" and Oct 2021 "ETH-Binance Bridge" are the **historical foundation** for today's Zoo-Lux bridge protocol and omnichain teleport integration
