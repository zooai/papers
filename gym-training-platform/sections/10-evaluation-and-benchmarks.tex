Key observations:
\begin{itemize}
\item QLoRA achieves 99\% of full fine-tuning quality at 50\% memory
\item Training-Free GRPO matches or exceeds gradient-based GRPO
\item RLHF methods (DPO, GRPO) significantly outperform SFT alone
\end{itemize}
